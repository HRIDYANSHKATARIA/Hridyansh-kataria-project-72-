RYAN INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Ryan International School, Rohini Sec 11, G2 nestled in the vicinity of Rohini Sec 11 is a part of the Ryan International Group of Institutions. In its continual efforts to revolutionize the education scenario guided by the futuristic view of Dr. Augustine F. Pinto, Founder Chairman of Ryan Group, this institution was established in the year 1998 with the motto “Excellence in Education and All Round Development”. It is a co-educational school affiliated to C.B.S.E with the state-of-the-art infrastructure. We believe that along with the core school activities, it is imperative for an educational institution to inculcate in children, spiritual well being, self confidence and tolerance and we do it by incorporating various activities, within the curriculum, based on the concept of Multiple intelligence. Our effort is directed towards global education integrated with innovative use of technology to develop global citizens through a holistic approach.
